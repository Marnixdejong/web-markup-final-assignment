Our Menu

Explore our selection of specialty coffees, teas, pastries and light bites. Everything is prepared fresh daily with the finest ingredients.

Coffee

  • Espresso – A classic and bold shot of caffeine.
  • Americano – Espresso tempered with hot water for a smoother taste.
  • Cappuccino – Espresso topped with steamed milk and a generous foam.
  • Flat White – Silky micro‑foamed milk blended with espresso.
  • Caffè Latte – Espresso with plenty of steamed milk and a whisper of foam.

Teas & Other Drinks

  • English Breakfast Tea – A rich and full‑bodied black tea.
  • Green Tea – Refreshing sencha with grassy notes.
  • Herbal Infusion – A calming blend of chamomile and mint.
  • Chai Latte – Spiced tea with steamed milk and a hint of honey.
  • Hot Chocolate – Creamy cocoa topped with whipped cream.

Pastries & Snacks

  • Croissant – A flaky, buttery pastry baked fresh each morning.
  • Chocolate Brownie – Rich, gooey chocolate brownie with walnuts.
  • Lemon Cake – Moist lemon cake slice with a zesty glaze.
  • Ham & Cheese Sandwich – Served on a crusty baguette with Dijon mustard.
  • Spinach & Feta Quiche – Light savoury quiche with seasonal greens.

All items are subject to availability. Please ask our staff about vegan and gluten‑free options.
